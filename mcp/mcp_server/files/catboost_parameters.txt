CatBoost Parameters:

1. **Common Parameters** (Official Docs):
   - `learning_rate` (default: 0.1)
   - `depth` (default: 6)
   - `iterations` (default: 100)
   - `loss_function` (e.g., 'Logloss', 'CrossEntropy')
   - `l2_leaf_reg` (regularization)

2. **Hyperparameters** (GeeksforGeeks):
   - `bagging_temperature` (controls diversity)
   - `od_type` (overfitting detection)
   - `random_state` (reproducibility)

3. **Categorical Data Handling** (apxml.com):
   - `cat_features` (list of categorical columns)
   - `border_count` (split points for categorical features)

4. **Special Parameters** (Medium):
   - Bayesian parameters (weight calculation)
   - `task_type` (CPU/GPU)

Source: https://catboost.ai/docs/en/references/training-parameters/common, https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/catboost-parameters-and-hyperparameters/
